About

Trivia City started out as a simple scheduling project for my programming portfolio.

I'd been working in the trivia business since 2012, first as an emcee during my stand up comedy days, then as a human resources manager, and then finally as an operations manager when the company was sold to a national trivia company. I know I never heard of that either.

After helping with the transition, the new owners offered me a position as a regional manager where I worked for another two years.

As much as I loved the company and the niche that I fell into, I love even more making things more efficient and decided that I wanted to do a web application to make things easier to run a trivia company remotely. So here's the web app. It's not an actual trivia company. Unless you want cute puppies and kitties to host your trivia event.
